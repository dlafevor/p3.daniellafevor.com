How to Play
  1. Select your difficulty
  2. Click a square.
  3. If have not clicked a square containing a mine, that square and all squares ajoining it will turn blue. Squares with a number in them indicate that at least one mine is touching that square (the number corrispondes to the number of mines touching that square).
  4. You can mark a square as having a mine under it by alt+clicking on the square
  5. If you click a square containing a mine the game is over ... KABLOOIE!!!
